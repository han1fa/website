All About Sleep

Sleep is super super super great. I love sleep. Even though I don't get too much sleep I still love sleeping.

Sleep is AMAZING for your mental and physical health.

I can literally sleep for 12+ hours straight. Sleep is literally the best thing in the world.

Sleeping is Extremely Important

It is crucial to a persons health that they sleep for at least 8 hours a day. I wish my teachers would understand that, they don't realize how much sleep I lose due to their assignments.

I think that ALL schools should have a nap time. If students are given time to sleep for at least 2 hours in between their classes they will perform much better.

I bet my teachers get at least 8 hours of sleep themselves. Unfortunatley, for us students that isn't always possible, thank goodness for the weekends, if we don't get enough sleep our everyday lifestyle will be affected. Heres a list of things sleep deprivation affect.

  • Concentration
  • Thinking ability
  • Hand-eye-coordination
  • Ability to multitask
  • Appetite
  • Weight

Here's a few ways schools and teachers can assist students with getting enough sleep each week:

  1. Assigning long assignments a week before they're due.
  2. Allowing them break time for a few hours.
  3. Making sure they don't assign too many projects and assignments in the same week.
This website will give you some other ways you can personally improve your quality of sleep each night.
